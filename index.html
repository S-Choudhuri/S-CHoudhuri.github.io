Hi! I am a Ph.D. student majoring in Computer Science at Arizona State University, and a member of the NetXT research Lab. With a demonstrated history of working in Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Machine Learning, my current research interest is primarily focused on a realistic Domain Adaptation scenario for image classification with different degrees of shared label space assumptions between domains.
